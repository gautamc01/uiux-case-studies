## 📌 Project Overview
Neer Prakalp is a mobile application designed to simplify and streamline water tanker booking for residential, commercial, and industrial users. It connects users with local water suppliers, ensuring reliable, transparent, and efficient water delivery.

## ⏱ Duration
7 Weeks

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Current water supply systems are unreliable and inefficient, causing inconsistent availability and lack of transparency.

## 🎯 Goal
Design a user-friendly solution that enables:
- Real-time tanker booking  
- Transparent pricing  
- Order tracking  
- Efficient supplier management  

## 🧠 My Role
- User Research  
- UX Design  
- Wireframing & Prototyping  
- User Testing  

## 🛠 Tools
Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Google Forms, Miro

---

## 🔍 User Research
Conducted qualitative and quantitative research to understand:
- Difficulty in finding nearby suppliers  
- Lack of real-time tracking  
- Unreliable delivery  

---

## 👤 User Persona
**Raj Vishwanath (Restaurant Owner, Pune)**  
Needs reliable water supply to run business without interruption.

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Users need **fast & reliable delivery**
- Transparency builds trust
- Emergency booking is critical
